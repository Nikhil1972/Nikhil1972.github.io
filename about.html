About Me

Hello! I’m Nikhil Kumar, a passionate and results-driven Senior Research Analyst at Dreamlock MR Pvt. Ltd. With a deep interest in market trends, consumer behavior, and strategic decision-making, I’ve dedicated myself to uncovering data-driven insights that lead to impactful business outcomes.

I began my career as a Research Analyst at Dreamlock MR Pvt. Ltd. and, within 6 months, my commitment and analytical skills led to my promotion to Senior Research Analyst. This recognition fueled my drive to deliver even more valuable insights and contribute to the growth of both my clients and the company.

Currently, I serve as the Team Leader of Research Analysts, where I’m responsible for guiding and mentoring a talented team of analysts. Together, we strive to produce high-quality market research, utilizing the latest techniques and data analytics tools. Our goal is to provide clients with actionable insights that shape their strategies and help them stay ahead in the competitive market.
