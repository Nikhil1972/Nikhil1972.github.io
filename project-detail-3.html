Competitive Intelligence for B2B E-Commerce Platform

Project Overview

Client: B2B E-Commerce Platform

Project Duration: 4 Months

Location: Global

Project Description

This competitive intelligence analysis project involved a detailed examination of a B2B e-commerce platform’s competitors. By studying competitor pricing, product offerings, and customer feedback, I helped the client identify gaps in their own platform and improve their market positioning. The insights led to a stronger customer acquisition strategy and an enhanced user experience.

Research Methodology

  • Competitive Benchmarking: A thorough comparison of product offerings, pricing, and customer feedback across competitors in the B2B e-commerce space.
  • Market Research: Conducted surveys and online reviews to gain insights into customer satisfaction and unmet needs.
  • SWOT Analysis: A detailed SWOT analysis of the client’s position versus competitors in the B2B marketplace.
  • Pricing Analysis: Monitored competitor pricing strategies and promotions to help the client optimize their own pricing model.

Key Findings & Results

  • Competitor Weakness: Identified gaps in competitor product offerings, which provided the client with opportunities to enhance their features.
  • Improved Customer Satisfaction: The insights helped refine the user experience, leading to increased customer satisfaction and a 10% rise in engagement.
  • Pricing Strategy: Recommended adjustments to pricing, which improved market competitiveness and resulted in a 12% increase in sales.

Conclusion

The competitive intelligence gathered through this project enabled the client to significantly improve their product offerings, user experience, and pricing strategy, enhancing their position in the B2B e-commerce market.
